# Number Soup - Word Search

A fun and interactive word search game using numbers instead of letters! Find hidden words encoded as numbers in a grid.

## How to Play

1. **View the letter-to-number mapping** (A=1, B=2, C=3... Z=26) displayed at the top
2. **Click number cells** in sequence to form words
3. **Match the hidden words** - The game will highlight when you find one
4. **Complete all rounds** - 20 rounds with increasing difficulty!

## Features

- **Three Difficulty Levels:**
  - Easy: 10x10 grid with 20 simple words
  - Medium: 12x12 grid with 20 intermediate words  
  - Hard: 14x14 grid with 20 challenging words

- **20 Rounds:** Each difficulty has 20 complete rounds to play
- **15+ Words Per Grid:** Every grid contains 15+ hidden words
- **Multiple Directions:** Words placed horizontally, vertically, and diagonally
- **Visual Feedback:** Lines drawn when words are found
- **Progress Tracking:** See words found and remaining
- **Responsive Design:** Play on desktop, tablet, or mobile

# � Letter Scramble

A fun and interactive game where you decode numbers into letters to find hidden words!

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **View the encoded numbers** - Each number represents a letter (1=A, 2=B, 3=C... 26=Z)
2. **Decode the numbers** - Figure out what word the numbers represent
3. **Enter your word answer** and click Submit
4. **Score points** based on how quickly you get it right!

## 📊 Features

- **Three Difficulty Levels:**
  - 🟢 Easy: 3-5 letter words
  - 🟡 Medium: 6-12 letter words
  - 🔴 Hard: 9+ letter words

- **Score Tracking:** Keep track of your points and attempts
- **Hints:** Get a hint to help you decode the numbers
- **Responsive Design:** Play on desktop, tablet, or mobile
